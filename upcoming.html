11 Nights and 10 Days

Islamabad, Skardu, Gilgit and Hunza Valley Tour

  • Day 1:

    Whole day at the second most beautiful capital city in the world, Islamabad. We will tour Museums, Moneuments and Faisal Masjid

  • Day 2:

    Fly to Skardu, arrival and transfer to hotel. Explore surroundings

  • Day 3:

    Move to Gilgit. We will travel through Deosai plains Considered "Land of the giants" and the worlds second highest plateau

  • Day 4:

    Explore gilgit and surroundings

  • Day 5:

    We will move to and fall in love in hunza valley. Considered "heaven on earth" With A Life Expectancy Of Over 120 Years

  • Day 6:

    whole day excursion to Khunjerab pass (Pakistan-China border). During our trip, we will witness the majestic Passu Cones and Attabad lake. We will return back to Hunza and transfer to hotel

  • Day 6:

    Explore different valleys of Hunza and witness hopper glacier

  • Day 7:

    Explore different valleys of Hunza and witness hopper glacier

  • Day 8:

    Move back to gilgit, transfer to hotel. We will visit gilgit city and enjoy dinner

  • Day 9:

    Fly back to islamabad. Go out for shopping and have dinner at Monal
