Made in Pakistan

Pakistan is home of many beautiful places that blessed in their most natural form. Also called “The Land of Pure”, Pakistan is a place of exotic land. It’s hotter than many deserts in the world, but a large portion of the land is green and fertile as its soil is watered by huge rivers which come from the Mighty Himalayas

After serving 25 years in the Pakistani Army, traveled and deployed over 40 countries including all around Pakistan, Col. Imran (R) wanted to help others experience to serenity side of Pakistan. In 2017, IK Pakistan Tours was founded in Islamabad with a mission to offer unique traveling experiences


Malaysia

With help of some word of mouth and high demand of international tourists seeking ways to travel to Pakistan, in 2018 IK Pakistan Tours expanded into Malaysia. We began our operations by offering tours catered to international travelers from Malaysia



New York City

In 2019, IK Pakistan Tours expanded into New York City. We hosted our first tour catered to travelers from Malaysia and New York City



Col. Imran Kiyani (R)

Co-Founder, CEO
Islamabad, Pakistan

Colonel Imran retired after serving 25 years in the Pakistani Army. He founded IK Pakistan Tours to help promote tourism in his beloved home. Always fond of travelling, Col. Imran understands the needs of travelers and special experiences they seek. With quite some impressive credentials that include serving as a UN Peace Keeping officer in Crotia, instructor at Pakistan army school of mountain warfare and physical trainer to national army level teams, Col. Imran's mission now is to boost economy in his beloved country by promoting tourism.


Being deployed all around pakistan including the "unforgiven" Siachen Glacier for two years, Imran wants the world to see the hidden and unknown parts of of Pakistan still waiting to be discovered
