Col. Imran Kiyani (R)

Co-Founder, CEO
Pakistan

Col. Imran (R) retired after serving 25 years in the Pakistani Army. Imran founded IK Pakistan Tours to help promote tourism in his beloved home, Pakistan. Always fond of travelling, Imran has travelled over 40 countries and also has served as a UN Peace Keeping officer in Crotia.

Being deployed all around pakistan including the "unforgiven" Siachen Glacier for two years, Imran wanted the world to see the hidden and unknown parts of of Pakistan still waiting to be discovered. His mission became to promote tourism by offering one of kind traveling experiences in Pakistan.

Fahad J. Kiani

Co-Founder, Product Developer
New York City

Originally born in Pakistan, Fahad moved to New York when he was 10 years old. He returned back after 18 years. Upon returning, he wanted others to experience the serenity joy that he experienced and help other travelers find the similar connection

While studying computer science at City University of New York - John Jay College, Fahad decided he wanted to bring the vision of IK Pakistan Tour to life and developed the website as a class project
