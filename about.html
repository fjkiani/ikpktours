Our Story

Made in Pakistan

After serving 25 years in the Pakistani Army, Col. Imran Kiyani (R) decided he wanted to help others experience to serenity side of Pakistan many seem to believe doesn't exist

In 2017, IK Pakistan Tours was founded in Islamabad with a mission to promote tourism by offering one of a kind joyful traveling experiences in Pakistan


Malaysia

In 2018, we expanded into Malaysia and began our operations by offering tours catered to international travelers



New York City

In 2019, IK Pakistan Tours expanded into their third location, New York City


Col. Imran Kiyani (R)

Co-Founder, CEO
Pakistan

Col. Imran (R) retired in 2015 and founded IK Pakistan Tours. Always fond of traveling, Imran travelled over 40 countries and whole Pakistan.

Serving several tours in Northern Pakistan, Imran wanted the world to see the hidden and unknown parts that are still waiting to be discovered. His mission became to promote tourism by offering one of kind traveling experiences

Fahad J. Kiani

Co-Founder, Product Developer
New York City

Originally born in Pakistan, Fahad moved to New York at a young age but returned back after 18 years for a visit. Upon returning, he wanted others to experience the serenity joy that he experienced and help other travelers find the similar connection

While studying computer science at City University of New York, Fahad decided he wanted to bring the vision of IK Pakistan Tour to life and developed the website as a class project
