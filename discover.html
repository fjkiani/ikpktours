Discover Yourself In Pakistan

By traveling, our feet learn about new paths never taken before while our heart begins to beat to a different rhythm. The more time we spend away from familiar routes, away from our comfortable lives, the more we learn to embrace the unknown

Pakistan aka “land of the pure” is considered the unknown. Waiting to be discovered.


Build The Jugaad Mindset

Despite being devastated by tragedies, turmoil within government and a crippled economy, you will find the people of Pakistan embracing the Jugaad mindset. This mindset simply means to create solutions to problems with limited resources, to “hack”



Be Stunned By Natural Beauty Of Pakistan

Home of the Himalayas: Mount Everst and the earths highest peak

Khewra Salt Mines: Worlds largest second salt mine said to be discovered by Alexander the Great’s horse

Karakoram: One of the world's most geologically active areas. Connects Pakistan, India, and China. Home of K2, the second highest peak in the world, Siachen Glacier and referred to as the Eighth Wonder of the World



Discover The Valleys

Pakistan is one of the few countries with such a dynamic to include landscape, rivers, lakes, waterfalls, springs and glaciers all in one place

Hunza Valley Referred to as "Heaven On Earth". Described as a permanently happy land, isolated from the world with a life expectancy of over 120 years for residents

Naran - Kaghan Valley It is one of the most beautiful parts of northern areas in Pakistan. Elevated over 2500 meters above sea level, it is a famous resort for trekkers. The ice on the mountain tops never melts, even in months of June and July there are glaciers and mountains are covered with snow

Attabad Lake The beauty that formed from a disaster due to a massive landslide. At the time of its formation, the local residents had no idea that there would be a lake formed that would hold so much beauty and would become a famous destination for tourists. Attabad Lake offers a magical feeling of peace, serenity and calmness. Away from the city life, this site can soothe the mind and offer a sense of serenity
